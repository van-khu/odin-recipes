Omelette

Description

A delicious omelette recipe. Perfect for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Butter
  • Mozzarella
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk 3 eggs
  2. Turn stove to medium and add butter to pan.
  3. Once the butter has melted, add the eggs.
  4. Add the cheese, fold the egg over the cheese.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
