Lasagna

Description

A delicious lasagna recipe you can make any night of the week.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook lasagna sheets according to package instructions.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees C.
  3. Layer pasta sheet, tomato sauce, cheese, then spinach. Repeat 3 times..
  4. Bake covered for 30 minutes.
