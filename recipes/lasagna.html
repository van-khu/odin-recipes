Lasagna

Description

A delicious lasagna recipe you can make any night of the week.

Ingredients

    Lasagna sheets Tomato sauce Mozzarella Spinach

Steps

    Boil water and cook lasagna sheets according to package instructions. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees C. Layer pasta sheet, tomato sauce, cheese, then spinach. Repeat 3 times.. Bake covered for 30 minutes.
