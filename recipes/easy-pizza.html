Easy Pizza

Description

A delicious pizza recipe you can make any night of the week.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Toppings (mushrooms, pepperoni, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees C
  2. Roll out pizza dough
  3. Add tomato sauce and cheese.
  4. Add desired toppings.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes.
